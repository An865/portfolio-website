About Anne

With several years of business management and business ownership experience I have developed a strong work ethic and leadership skills. I am highly organized, responsible, and driven which has contributed to my successes in music, philosophy of language (semantics), library and information science, and web development.

My dedication to the study of the philosophy of language, formal logic, and formal semantics at the University of Arizona has lead to a publication with Oxford University Press.

Having studied classical guitar performance at the University of Arizona and flamenco guitar in Granada, Spain I have enjoyed a semi-professional career offering flamenco guitar instruction.

Having merged my passion for formal semantics with my interests in web technologies I am interested in current web technologies. I have experience with current technology like HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript as well as the future of the web, namely semantic technologies and principles such as OWL, RDF, ontologies, and Description Logic.

As a web developer I strive to carefully listen to clients' needs and help them decide on a vision for their online presence.

I seek out opportunities to help non-profit and grassroots organizations.
